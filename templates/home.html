About Us

We started this project as a response to the increasing amount of hate speech on the internet. Our goal is to make the internet a safer place for everyone, regardless of their race, gender, or beliefs.

As Holberton students, we developed this project as part of our curriculum. You can find the source code on our GitHub repository.
